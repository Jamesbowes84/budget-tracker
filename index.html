Connect Dropbox Sync
One-time setup. After this, your budget auto-saves and loads from Dropbox every time you open the app on any device.
Setup steps:
1
Go to dropbox.com/developers/apps and sign in
2
Click Create app → Choose Scoped access → App folder → name it anything (e.g. "BudgetTracker") → Create app
3
Go to Permissions tab → check files.content.read and files.content.write → Submit
4
Go to Settings tab → scroll to OAuth 2 → click Generate under "Generated access token" → copy it
5
Go to Settings tab → scroll to OAuth 2 → Redirect URIs → Add https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io → Save
6
Paste your token below and click Connect
Your token is stored only in your browser. Data saves to a private app folder in your Dropbox — no one else can access it.
